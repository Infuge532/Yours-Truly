About Christina

Designing spaces that feel like home

Founder of Yours Truly, Interior Design LLC — bringing warmth, intention, and over a decade of expertise to every project in the Kansas City area.

The Story

A passion for beautiful spaces

Christina Ryan has spent her career doing what she loves: transforming rooms into spaces that feel intentional, beautiful, and completely authentic to the people who live and work in them. She studied interior design and has spent over a decade applying that training to real projects — from intimate home renovations to large commercial spaces — across the Kansas City metro.

Her work spans a wide range of styles: the warmth of a farmhouse kitchen, the cool sophistication of a contemporary basement, the functional elegance of a clubhouse interior. Whatever the style, the throughline is always the same — Christina listens first, then designs.

She founded Yours Truly, Interior Design LLC because she believes great design isn't about imposing a look — it's about uncovering the space's personality and bringing it to life. When you work with Christina, the result is never generic. It's yours. Truly.

Experience & Credentials

Trained. Certified. Passionate.
