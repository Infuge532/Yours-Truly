About Christina

Design with a personal touch

Christina Ryan founded Yours Truly, Interior Design LLC with one goal: to create spaces that feel genuinely, unmistakably you. With over a decade of experience across residential and commercial projects in the Kansas City area, she brings formal design training, sharp instincts, and a warm, collaborative approach to every project.

Certified in home staging since 2017, Christina has helped homeowners sell faster and for more — while also helping others fall back in love with the homes they already have.
